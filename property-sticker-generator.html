Before you start — prepare a clean Excel file

For the system to read your data correctly, the Excel file must be clean and headerless:

  • Row 1 must be the column names only (e.g. Property No., Article, Description, Model/Serial No., Amount, Date of Acquisition, Issued to).
  • No title rows above the data — remove things like "Republic of the Philippines", "PhilFIDA", office numbers, addresses, or contact details.
  • No images — no logos, signatures, or scanned photos in the sheet.
  • No merged cells or blank spacer rows — just plain columns of information.

In short: one header row, then the data. Nothing else.
